# Super Store Sales Data Analysis Power BI Dashboard
### 1.	Overview 
Super Store Sales Data Analysis Dashboard :
A dynamic, interactive data visualization tool built to get insights from the sales data of a Super Store operating in all North America region.

### 2.	Objective of the Dashboard 
To contribute to the success of a SuperMarket or Superstore by analyzing their sales data using various data analysis techniques, especially focusing on time series analysis to provide valuable business insights and accurate sales forecasting.

Objectives can be broken down into the following parts : 

* **Dashboard Creation**: Identify the KPIs, design an intuitive and visually appealing dashboard, and add interactive visualizations and filtering capabilities to allow users to explore the data at various levels of granularity. <br> 
* **Data Analysi**: Provide valuable insights to the business entities about the effectiveness of their sales strategies through various charts and data visualizations. <br>
* **Sales Forecasting**: Leverage historic data and apply time series analysis to generate sales forecasts for the next 30 days. <br>
* **Actionable Insights and Recommendations**: The end goal is to share valuable insights and actionable information that can drive strategic decision-making and support Superstorr‚Äôs goal for growth, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

### 4.	Dataset

* Data is in the form of .csv file named as SuperStore_Sales_Dataset.csv downloaded from Kaggle .

* This dataset has almost 6k rows containing information about the orders placed by customers , geographic information of the store and shipping mode over a period of two years from 2019 to 2020. 
* Along with this a new calculated table was also created about the sales forecast. 


### 5. Key Insights from the Dashboard 
* Total Sales = $ 1.6 M
* Total Profit = $ 175 K 
* Total Quantity Sold = 22 K 
* Average Delivery Time = 3.96 days 
* Office Supplies category items achieved highest sales followed by Technology and Furniture .
* 43% of the sales came from COD followed by Cards and then Online as Payment modes.
* The sales and profit figures were higher in 2020 as compare to 2019.
* Maximum sales happened Western region followed by East , Central and South of America.
* Sales forecasting for next 30 days was calculted with the help of Line Chart and clearly visualized in the forecasting page of the dashboard .
